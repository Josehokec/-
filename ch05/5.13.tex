\problem{证明: $S=\{a_1,a_2,\cdots,a_k\}$是Turing-可计算的.}

\begin{proof}

{\color {red} {对第一版答案做出了修正.}}
构造${\chi}_S = N^2 \big( \prod ^k_{i=1}(x \dotdiv a_i) \big)$,于是有：

$${\chi}_S=
\protect\begin{cases}
    0, & \textrm{若}x \in S,\\
    1, & \textrm{否则}.
\protect\end{cases}$$

故${\chi}_S \in \EF$.由定理5.18(page \  138)知${\chi}_S$是Turing-可计算的.

\end{proof}
